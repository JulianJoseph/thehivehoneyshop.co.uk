March 02, 2005

FHM, For Him magazine Special Promotion with The Hive

FHM Magazine has enlisted the help of The Hive Honey Shop to help promote their new March cover girl. The competition, known as 'Finding the 2005 High Street Honey', gave way to FHM contacting The Hive, asking them to bottle special limited edition honey featuring a photo of Natalie, the winner, in her bra. 'It has never been done before' said the managing director, but The Hive agreed and now have these jars on sale in their shop and on-line. ' They are making heads turn and are a very pleasant sight at breakfast first thing in the morning.'
